About Me

I was born on a crisp garbage day in April 1983. I was a creative and sleepy child who grew up to be a creative and sleepy adult. I have always wanted to have a career in a creative field, and chose IMD after working at my dad's graphic design company for five years. I learnt so much from the other designers there but most of them started their careers in the age of Letraset and x-acto knives - so off to school I went.

When I'm not at school you can find me sewing, knitting, reading or working part-time as a server. I live in Little Italy with my partner, our cat Lambic and a basset hound named Blanche. I love learning new creative things and most recently took a stained glass class. Next I would love to learn shoemaking and millinery! But for now, I will focus my attention on learning the basics of html... 😜
